# End-To-End-data-analytics-python-sql-powerbi
An end-to-end data analytics project covering data cleaning, Python, EDA, SQL analysis, and Power BI dashboarding to uncover customer behavior insights and support data-driven decisions.

🔍 Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer purchasing behavior using end-to-end data analytics techniques. It involves data extraction, cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), SQL-based querying, and building an interactive Power BI dashboard to derive business insights.

The goal is to understand customer trends such as purchasing patterns, revenue distribution, and customer segmentation to support data-driven decision-making.

📁 Dataset

The dataset contains customer transaction and demographic information, including:

Customer ID
Location
Age Group
Product Category
Purchase Amount
Subscription Status
Shipping Type
Review Ratings

💼 Business Insights
High-Value Customer Segment:
Middle-aged customers contribute the highest share of revenue, making them the most valuable segment.
Top Performing Category:
Accessories generate the highest sales, indicating strong customer demand in this category.
Geographical Trends:
Certain locations (e.g., New York, New Mexico) show higher purchase volumes, highlighting regional demand differences.
Customer Engagement:
Subscription-based customers show consistent purchasing behavior and better engagement.
Low Performing Segment:
Senior customers contribute the least revenue, indicating lower engagement or unmet needs.

📌 Recommendations
Target High-Value Segments:
Focus marketing campaigns and personalized offers on middle-aged customers to maximize revenue.
Boost Low-Performing Segments:
Introduce targeted discounts or simplified user experiences for senior customers to increase engagement.
Expand Top Categories:
Increase inventory and promotions for accessories to capitalize on high demand.
Location-Based Marketing:
Run region-specific campaigns in high-performing areas and improve outreach in low-performing regions.
Enhance Subscription Strategy:
Promote subscription plans with exclusive benefits to improve customer retention and repeat purchases.
Optimize Product Mix:
Analyze underperforming categories and adjust pricing, promotions, or product offerings.
